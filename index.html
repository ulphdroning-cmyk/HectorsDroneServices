Frequently Asked Questions

Is drone flying legal in residential areas?

Yes. All flights are conducted in full compliance with UK CAA regulations and local safety requirements.

What happens if the weather is bad?

If conditions are unsafe for flying, we reschedule at no extra cost.

How long does editing take?

Most projects are delivered within 48–72 hours.

Do I need permission from neighbours?

In most cases, no — but we always assess the site and advise if any additional permissions are required.
